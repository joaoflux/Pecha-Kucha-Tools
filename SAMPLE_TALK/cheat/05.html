• The important thing is that you add your cheat notes to the same directory as your slide images
  • And that they are well named (01.html, 02.html, etc.)
  • In case you're not used to coding: It's a good idea to use plain text editors (Note Pad, Text Edit)
  • Using word processers like MS Word, Pages, Libre Office is a bad idea
  • Because you may end up with hidden formatting and broken characters
